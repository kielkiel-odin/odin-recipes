Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Béchamel sauce (butter, flour, milk)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking Process:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté chopped onions and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper, and simmer.
  5. Prepare béchamel sauce by melting butter, adding flour, and gradually whisking in milk until smooth and thick.
  6. In a baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  7. Repeat layers and finish with a generous layer of cheese on top.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 30-40 minutes, until golden and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool slightly before serving.
